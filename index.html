Trézor.io/start Suite App (Official) | Trezor Hardware Wallet

The Trézor.io/start Suite App is the official application for managing your Trezor hardware wallet. It provides a user-friendly interface to help you securely manage your cryptocurrencies, perform transactions, and access various features of your Trezor device.

Getting Started with Trézor.io/Start

To get started with Trézor.io/Start, follow these simple steps:

  • Visit the Trézor.io/Start website.
  • Download the Trezor Suite app for your operating system.
  • Install the app and connect your Trezor device to your computer.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to set up your wallet and secure it with a PIN and recovery seed.

How do I access my Trezor?

Accessing your Trezor is straightforward:

  • Connect your Trezor device to your computer using the provided USB cable.
  • Open the Trezor Suite app.
  • Enter your PIN code on the Trezor device when prompted.
  • Once authenticated, you can access your wallet and manage your cryptocurrencies.

Is Trezor 100% safe?

Trezor is designed to provide the highest level of security for your cryptocurrencies. While no system can be guaranteed to be 100% safe, Trezor employs advanced security measures to protect your assets, including:

  • Secure PIN protection to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Recovery seed phrase for wallet recovery in case of device loss or damage.
  • Regular firmware updates to address potential vulnerabilities.
  • Open-source software for transparency and community auditing.
